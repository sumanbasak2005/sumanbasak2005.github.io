Who I am

About Me

I am a Lead Site Reliability and DevOps Engineer with nearly two decades of experience in payment infrastructure, including 10+ years at Visa Inc. I specialize in architecting and operating large-scale distributed payment platforms — from microservices transformation and AI-driven SSDLC automation to end-to-end observability and zero-trust security.

My work spans the full lifecycle of global payment systems: I have led engineering on Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay, Visa Direct, Tokenization, Click to Pay, and Paze, coordinating geographically distributed SRE teams across North America, APAC, and India.

Beyond engineering, I contribute to the broader technology community as an IEEE Senior Member, IETE Fellow, and active peer reviewer for IEEE and ACM publications. My research focuses on AI agents for secure software development, observability engineering, and the future of agentic payment systems.
